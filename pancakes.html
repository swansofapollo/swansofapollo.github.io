Russian pancakes recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 0.5 teaspoons of salt
  • 500-700 ml of whole milk
  • 0.5 teaspoons of soda slaked with vinegar or baking powder
  • 2 glasses of flour

The recipe is kinda simple. You need to mix it all together. And fry it. LOL
